In almost silence, where echoes dwell,  
Between the heartbeats of the chapel bells,  
Time folds its wings, the sky veils arc,  
Reveal the pulsing drums, of worlds apart  

Matrimony, oh mysterious art,  
Where shadows dance and beginnings start,  
Lift the lovers beyond our sight,  
To overstand, aglow with light.  

Here, the simple touch of hand in palm  
Becomes a serenading song of psalm,  
Where love, redeemed, begins to sing,  
Affection earns another wing.  

Protect us, wrap us, hold us tight  
Swaddle, cuddle, give us flight  
Beyond we soar, past auld lang syne,  
Where the ancients dance with witches wine.  

For this is the lovers' secret creed,  
That love is found not in the deed,  
But in the tapestry that binds, unseen,  
The worlds of all, both past and keen.  

Matron roots, mystery profound,  
In you, our earthly souls are bound,  
Lifted by life's transforming grace,  
We dance again in lovers' space.
