Every now and again
The faint slience catches my ear
The cackle of the needle
Edging too and fro'

Circling...

But never much closer to the spindle

Plateauing...

THe sounds from yesterday
Echo from the pavillion

What happened to the music?
To what song do we dance now?
Where are the ones who's fiddles set us in motion?
Will you return?

For the record
Sounds no more
But a faint silence
That everyone now and again
Catches my ear
