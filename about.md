# About

Threaded Poets Society is an open gathering for poems and the people who write them.

The name comes from an old idea: that the poet is a needle and thread, stitching the living to the dead, the spoken to the unspeakable, the self to everything it has loved and lost.

These poems are offered freely, because a poem belongs to whoever needs it. They are written here because writing is one of the ways some of us stay honest — with ourselves, with the people we love, with the world as it actually is.

The project is alive. Adding a poem requires only a file and a push. There is no editor, no committee, no submission window. Only the poem, and the decision to make it visible.

If poetry calls to you — to write it, to read it, to sit quietly inside it — you belong here. The thread continues.
