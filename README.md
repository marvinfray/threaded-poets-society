# Threaded Poets Society

## Purpose
The Threaded Poets Society is a collaborative and inclusive space dedicated to reviving and sustaining the poet within us all.  In a world where creativity often takes a backseat to productivity, our mission is to reconnect individuals with the art of poetry as a means of reflection, expression, and connection.  By leveraging LLM tools alongside timeless poetic traditions, we aim to democratize creativity and inspire collective wonder.

## Mission
To cultivate a global community of poets and creatives who:
- Celebrate the transformative power of poetry.
- Collaborate on writing and sharing poems that resonate with the human experience.
- Leverage AI as a tool for creativity and exploration, not a replacement for the human voice.
- Foster sustainability in poetic expression, ensuring its relevance for future generations.

## Vision
A world where poetry is accessible, collaborative, and thriving—a shared language that bridges cultures, ideas, and experiences. By combining traditional poetic methods with emerging technologies, we envision a living, breathing poetic revival that inspires and sustains.

## Goals
1. **Create Together:** Facilitate collaborative poetry writing sessions using AI tools like ChatGPT to enhance creativity.
2. **Open Source Poetry:** Host our collective work on an open-source GitHub repository, inviting global contributions and dialogue.
3. **Publish a Book:** Within 12 months, compile our best works into a published anthology that tells the story of our journey.
4. **Community Building:** Foster a respectful, inclusive environment where members can explore, reflect, and grow through poetry.
5. **Sustainability:** Develop practices and discussions around what sustains poetry and how poetry sustains us, aligning with themes of interconnectedness and the cycles of life.

## How to Get Involved
1. **Join the Conversation:** Participate in our online discussions and poetry prompts.
2. **Collaborate:** Submit, edit, and co-create poems using AI tools and your own unique voice.
3. **Contribute to the Repository:** Share your poetry, reflections, or ideas in our open-source GitHub project.
4. **Spread the Word:** Encourage others to join the Living Poets Society, making poetry a shared experience.

## Why AI?

AI tools like ChatGPT allow us to reimagine poetic collaboration, offering:
- A way to bridge the gap between tradition and innovation.
- Deeper introspection and broader connection.
- An elemement of (perhaps unsettling) mystery.  

AI serves as a partner, not a replacement—enhancing human creativity while honoring its roots. This project also builds on the vision of [Wundr Space](https://www.wundr.space/wundr-ing-space), which explores the intersections of technology, consciousness, and creativity. In these unsettling times, let us gather as a collective, with poetry as our torch, navigating the intricate maze of possibilities alongside the burgeoning force of artificial intelligence. 

The "ground zero cohort" of living poets will connect and collaborate using https://www.communi.team/. 

## The Name
The Threaded Poets Society nods to the enduring legacy of poetry and the vital, ongoing work of keeping it alive and relevant. It reflects our commitment to being a "needle and thread" that connects the living to the dead, through poetry. 

## Let’s Begin
If poetry inspires you — to create, to reflect, to connect — then you belong here. Together, we’ll write, share, and sustain the poetry of life.
