[**The Beginishing**](https://open.spotify.com/track/2SCLswvRP8hAYdIKsrLV6h?si=ad3bc2ebed79437a)

[You know you have to go](https://open.spotify.com/track/62ZDZ7CMf0j1lEmTvRknGu?si=7663aad3f9fc4f03) —   
we've known it long enough  
to pack the knowing gently,  
fold it between the things you'll carry.  

[A horse with no name](https://open.spotify.com/track/54eZmuggBFJbV7k248bTTt?si=381b0cf140574d79) is still a horse.  
A love with no address still sends letters  
in frequencies only skin remembers.  

So go. Go where the [black sands](https://open.spotify.com/track/6uoM7KHsyJsHZqBQHKjQbc?si=695a606fdd8442ba)  
meet a sea that doesn't know my name for it,  
where the nights come differently  
and the bloom of something unfamiliar  
opens against the heat of a season  
that forgot to ask permission.  

I will [stay awake](https://open.spotify.com/track/3UAeADJJPX2IFYpGqypsBZ?si=a44a3da7f96d4bdc).  
I will be the shore that waits for tide —  
the [scientist](https://open.spotify.com/track/4W7f4EzaVPJfLvmYwls6l6?si=c69d2a4a6f9b4273) of your absence,  
measuring the half-[life](https://open.spotify.com/track/61o9ek0A26KARnXBPssDv3?si=5e7b16effe2a4f3f) of a morning  
[without you](https://open.spotify.com/track/32CFVYfYT4wE1MTNWj66Rt?si=db1c4190927e497e) in it,  
watching light behave  
as if it doesn't know it's being watched.  

[Perth](https://open.spotify.com/track/6oeuEHxwfbqu5xIJrcbPzN?si=866a10384adc4c3b) is a long way from here.  
Every place you'll sleep is.  
But the [archangel](https://open.spotify.com/track/6evpAJCR5GeeHDGgv3aXb3?si=5c3d84953c034813) of the small hours  
visits us both —  
in whatever timezone mercy sends —  
and I have found  
[what I'll use](https://open.spotify.com/track/5zT5cMnMKoyruPj13TQXGx?si=376e991ae7c143db) as lighthouse:  
the thought of you  
still breathing somewhere,  
still laughing —  
the kind of laughing  
that doesn't need me in the room,  
that belongs to the space between people  
who love each other in the dark,  
who find each other funny  
in the face of all this enormity.  

Beneath all this —  
beneath the trip-hop pulse  
of all our long slow evenings,  
the [bloom](https://open.spotify.com/track/0vtX8UMG38p7IXpP4lZJ2z?si=70d9b0d2c5934c29) and the [black](https://open.spotify.com/track/5Xak5fmy089t0FYmh3VJiY?si=357e47c482eb483f)
and the luminous between —  
there is a frequency  
only we are tuned to.  

Play it from the other side of the world.
I'll [feel it all around](https://open.spotify.com/track/79H5IKTwXdAbqypigoP832?si=ec10884fe9d149ea).

We made this:  
[a playlist strung between latitudes](https://open.spotify.com/playlist/0JDXzWeL79OMxn9GvM6rmi?si=DTEkU2JBS_SemSIpolPZZw)  
like washing on a line —  
each song a different weight and texture,  
each one something we held once  
and said *yes. this one. keep it.*  

The [nights](https://open.spotify.com/track/7eqoqGkKwgOaWNNHx90uEZ?si=4a961755d9d7474c) fall [twice](https://open.spotify.com/track/4GGw2WhvFOniMdAl8ZLpVW?si=34efcbb0f55c45f1):  
once where you are,  
once where I am,  
the same [moon](https://open.spotify.com/track/5i2TbMNxIUarbfTIDGxhAs?si=a965ef47ab944ec3) doing double shifts,  
the same stars pulling overtime  
above whichever shore you've found.  

Distance is not absence.  
It is only the water  
between two tides  
that already know their way home.  

[*Stay forever*](https://open.spotify.com/track/7ln53hzSdrXrucU2gMlWTt?si=6aaf8b17a6eb4e93)  
was never something I could ask.  
But come back.  

Come back carrying whatever the [sea](https://open.spotify.com/track/25lM7zxYOd0YTd1DwfkQQu?si=9b4d80257ec04de5) gave you —  
whatever the vastness laughed you into,  
whatever new thing opened  
in the heat and strangeness of it.  

I will [hang on](https://open.spotify.com/track/4PLOJDcUb3gwfMLoZPQt3O?si=fece7b72cf644f0e).  
I will hang on  
the way the shore hangs on  
to the shape the tide has made of it —  

changed by the going,  
held by the returning,  

and always,  
always,  
under the same [blanket of stars](https://open.spotify.com/track/3T1fDrW1P0RLTruujNPrKl?si=97f90079695d40d0).
