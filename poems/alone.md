---
title: Alone
date: 2025-01-11
tags: [loss, solitude, sea]
commit_note: "the slow withdrawal of closeness; a tide that takes without intention, leaving the shore clutching at nothing"
---

Clasping at your attention
Once within reach
Drifting away

The tide has the better of us both
It's drag pulls without grip
Placing ever more distance between us

Breath easy
Gentle are the waves
But the undercurrent promise won't break

Wreckage on the shore
Clambering at nothing
Alone ever more
