---
title: Catch My Ear
date: 2025-01-22
tags: [music, silence, loss]
commit_message: "Written some time in 2022 (not dated in my book). From a time when I recognised a deeper sense of nostalgia and longing arising in myself. I was wrestling with the grief of loss, days gone by, days that never came, and the passage of time. It reflects on the faint silence of my yesterdays, the echoes from the yesterdays that preceded mine and the continuous circling needle of the great song of life and death."
footnote: "the silence left by vanished music; a record that has reached its end and the question of what holds us in motion when the ones who set us dancing are gone"
---

Every now and again
The faint slience catches my ear
The cackle of the needle
Edging too and fro'

Circling...

But never much closer to the spindle

Plateauing...

THe sounds from yesterday
Echo from the pavillion

What happened to the music?
To what song do we dance now?
Where are the ones who's fiddles set us in motion?
Will you return?

For the record
Sounds no more
But a faint silence
That everyone now and again
Catches my ear
